The Planet Jupiter

Mass and size

Jupiter's mass is 2.5 times that of all the other planets in the Solar System combined

Internal structure

It is estimated that the core is 30–50% of the planet's radius, and contains heavy elements 7–25 times the mass of Earth.

Atmosphere

Jupiter has the deepest planetary atmosphere in the Solar System

Magnetosphere

Jupiter's magnetic field is fourteen times stronger than Earth's, ranging from 4.2 gauss

Orbit and rotation

Jupiter is the only planet whose barycentre with the Sun lies outside the volume of the Sun
